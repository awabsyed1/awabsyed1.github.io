Professional Journey

My journey in the world of engineering began with Mechanical engineering. I developed deep fascination for the intricate workings of machinery and a keen eye for understanding how things are put together. This broaden my engineering horizon from microscale of tiny gears to macroscale of industrial machinery.

With natural curiosity for electrifying the world, I ventured into electrical and control engineering during my undergraduate engineering with the focus on designing speed control systems for Tesla SP85. My journey took an exciting turn when I delved into control and systems engineering, with the focus on robotics. This discipline allowed me apply my knowledge of various engineering facets - Mechanical, Electrical, Controls and Software engineering to create systems that operate seamlessly and autonomously. My work in this field has led to innovations in automation, robotics, and autonomous vehicles.
